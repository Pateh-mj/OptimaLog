Capabilities

Everything your team needs

From the individual contributor logging their day, to the supervisor seeing the full picture — OptimaLog covers every layer of your team's activity.

Activity Logging

Employees describe their work, tag a project, attach an image, and mark entries as reusable knowledge — all from a clean side-by-side form. Draft auto-saves as you type.

Shared Knowledge Base

Every "Save as Knowledge" log feeds into a team-wide KB. Search by keyword, filter by category, open entries in a slide-out reader. Building institutional knowledge, daily.

Admin Analytics

Real-time KPIs: total logs, active staff count, KB contributions, and participation rate. Filter by date and department. Includes hourly activity distribution and project breakdown chart.

Streaks & Weekly Strip

A 7-day strip shows logging consistency at a glance. Consecutive-day streaks are tracked and displayed with a live fire badge — reinforcing healthy daily habits.

Announcements

Admins broadcast organisation-wide notices. Pin critical ones to the top. Employees see announcements on their dashboard the moment they log in — no email required.

Staff Management

Edit user profiles, reset passwords, and manage roles and departments from a clean admin table. Export filtered activity logs to CSV for external reporting.

Workflow

Simple by design

Three steps from zero to full team visibility.

1

Register & Sign In

Create an account with your name, department, and role. The admin assigns supervisor or admin access where needed. Sign in securely from any browser.

2

Log Your Work Daily

Describe your task, pick a project, optionally attach an image. Check "Save as Knowledge" to contribute to the shared KB. Ctrl+Enter submits instantly.

3

Supervisors See It All

The admin panel shows live KPIs, per-employee analytics, hourly distribution, activity history with full-text search, and knowledge leaders — filter by date, department, or type.

Roles

Built for every role

Every user gets a focused, role-tailored experience the moment they sign in.

Employee
Daily contributor

Your personal workspace for logging, tracking your progress, and staying informed on what matters.

Side-by-side log form & activity history
Log on the left, today's and past activity on the right. Switch between Today and 7-day History tabs without a page reload.
7-day strip with streak tracking
See your week at a glance. A fire badge shows your current consecutive-day streak — building a consistent logging habit.
Draft auto-save & image attachment
Your in-progress log is saved to local storage as you type. Attach screenshots or work evidence directly to an entry.
Announcements & knowledge base access
Org-wide announcements appear on your dashboard. Browse the team knowledge base to find reusable procedures, notes, and lessons learned.
Supervisor / Admin
Team oversight

Full visibility into team performance, knowledge growth, staff data, and the ability to broadcast to the organisation.

Live KPI dashboard with hourly chart
Logs filed, active staff, KB contributions, and participation rate — all filterable by date and department. An hourly bar chart shows when the team is most active.
Per-employee analytics table
For any given day: each employee's log count, KB articles, first and last log time, active span in minutes, and their most-used project.
Searchable activity log + CSV export
Browse all logs with filters by type, department, and keyword. Click any row to open the full entry in a slide-out panel. Export any filtered view to CSV.
Staff management & password reset
Edit names, emails, roles, and departments. Reset passwords and generate temporary credentials instantly. Delete accounts with all their associated logs.
